WHO WE ARE

WHO WE ARE

Here at Coders without borders, we believe in inclusion by connecting with like-minded individuals and learning new skills. That is why we teach people with refugee and migrant background coding and digital skills with the hope that by giving them equal opportunities for an education they'll build a better future for themselves.

START A CAREER IN IT!

Why you should start to learn it? IT is a fast-growing profession with up to 20% higher salaries than other professions. A deficit of IT professionals in the whole of Europe makes it easier to find a job. You can work in a company or freelance from anywhere in the world.

BEGINNERS

In our beginner course you'll learn about basics of
HTML and CSS. you'll get familiar with the concept of
coding. In the end of the course you'll be able to build a simple
responsive website.

INTERMEDIATE

You'll get assignment in for of a little project that you'll
do with a help of your teachers/mentors. You can later
use the work you did to put it in your CV and portfolio.

ADVANCED

You'll learn JavaScript programming language with our
teachers who work in Croatian IT companies so you'll
have a chance to learn about modern tools and ways
of working as well. In the end of the course you'll be
able to build more complex interactive websites and
write clean code which is very important in your future
workplace.

DIGITAL LITERACY

In this course you'll learn and/or improve your digital
skills suchs as using google search, using google
sheets, what is the Internet, what is a website, social
networks and how to use them for business... if you're
not familiar with the digital world thiscan be a first
step for getting involved in our other, more complex
courses.
